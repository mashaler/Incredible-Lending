⚠ One-time setup needed This form needs a free Formspree account to send you emails. Here is how to set it up:
  1. Go to formspree.io and sign up free
  2. Click New Form, give it a name like "LendTrack Applications"
  3. Copy your form ID — it looks like xabc1234 (the part after /f/ in the endpoint URL)
  4. Open this HTML file in a text editor, find YOUR_FORM_ID and replace it with your ID
  5. Save and re-open — done! Every submission emails you instantly.
